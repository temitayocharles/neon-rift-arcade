Neon Rift

A fast, side-scrolling arcade action game built in one HTML file. Move through a neon arena, dodge hostile drones, chain pulse attacks, and survive escalating waves. No external assets, no install step.

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
Action: Space or Left Click
Dash: Shift
Pause: P

Tip: collect cyan cores for shield, gold cores for rapid pulses, and purple cores for score multiplier.
